The Journey

ORIGIN

Sri Lankan Highlands

It Begins in the Mist.

Not in a factory, but on the steep slopes of Ratnapura. The soil, the rain, and the altitude create a terroir that cannot be replicated. We go to the source.

CRAFT

The Artisan's Hand

Generations of Skill.

Knowledge passed down in a single touch. Hand-peeled cinnamon quills. Hand-selected clove buds. We preserve the imperfect, beautiful human art of harvesting.

WILD

Untamed Forests

Nature Has No Recipe.

From the saline mangroves of the Sundarbans to the deep woods of the Nilgiris. Our honey is foraged, not farmed. Raw, unheated, and speaking of the wild.

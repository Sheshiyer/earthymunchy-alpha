Inclusion #1 Herbal

Tulsi Honey

Sacred Basil Blooms • Northern India

Bees forage on wild Krishna Tulsi flowers. The result is a deep amber honey with distinct herbal notes and a calming aroma. Revered in Ayurveda for immunity.

Included in The Vault

Inclusion #2 Fruity

Lychee Honey

Muzaffarpur Orchards • Bihar

Light, almost translucent. It carries the unmistakable floral sweetness of Lychee blossoms. A favorite for teas and desserts as it doesn't overpower.

Included in The Vault

Inclusion #3 Nutty

Moringa Honey

Miracle Tree • Rajasthan

Dark, robust, and distinctively nutty. Sourced from the white flowers of the Moringa tree in arid regions. Packed with antioxidants.

Included in The Vault

Inclusion #4 Smoky

Red Forest

Western Ghats • Wild Flora

Harvested from the high-altitude forests. This honey is bold, viscous, and has notes of molasses and smoke. Not for the faint of heart.

Included in The Vault

Inclusion #5

Black Forest

Deep, intense flavor similar to honeydew. Sourced from aphids on pine and oak trees.

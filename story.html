The Founder's Journey

One backpack.
Fifty farms.

It started with a simple question: Where does my food actually come from?

I spent six months traveling through the hill country of Sri Lanka and the forests of India. Not staying in hotels, but in the homes of farmers. I learned that real cinnamon isn't just bark—it's a craft perfected over centuries by families initiated into the art.

Earthy Munchy isn't a corporation. It's a bridge. A direct line from the hands that harvest to the hands that cook.
